Biography

SAHILKUMAR GOSALIYA

If you've made it this far, you must be curious to know more about me.

Well, I have been a freelancer for the past 3 years. I fluently use Adobe Illustrator for my work, whether it's designing logos or creating fan art for comics and movies.

With a Bachelor's in Computer Science, I have an understanding of computing concepts and coding across various languages.

Being a certified Interactive Media Designer, I design and create responsive websites, motion graphics, and also enjoy video editing. All of these works include the profound use of Adobe Suite, Cinema 4D, and VS Code.

I am an ambitious, creative, and goal-oriented individual with some introverted tendencies. My downtime involves visiting movies and museums, and when I'm not engaged in social interaction, I indulge in reading and writing fictional novels.

If you are a fan of George R. R. Martin, J. K. Rowling, or Stan Lee, we could easily become friends!

Biography/ About and Video

If you've reached this point, you're likely curious to learn more about me. Over the past 3 years, I've worked as a freelancer, proficiently utilizing Adobe Illustrator for various projects. Whether it's crafting logos or producing fan art for comics and movies, I'm dedicated to delivering high-quality designs that exceed expectations.

With a Bachelor's in Computer Science, I possess a solid grasp of computing concepts and proficiency in coding across multiple programming languages. Additionally, as a certified Interactive Media Designer, I specialize in designing and developing responsive websites, creating captivating motion graphics, and indulging in the art of video editing. My expertise spans the use of Adobe Suite, Cinema 4D, and VS Code, ensuring the delivery of impactful and innovative projects

I am a motivated and focused individual with a passion for creativity and achieving goals. While I may lean towards introversion, I channel my energy into productive activities. During my leisure time, I enjoy exploring cinema and art at museums, as well as immersing myself in literature through reading and writing fictional novels.

If you share an appreciation for the works of esteemed authors such as George R. R. Martin, J. K. Rowling, or Stan Lee, I believe we could have common ground to build upon.
